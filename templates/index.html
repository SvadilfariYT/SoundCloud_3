A spectrogram is a visual representation of the frequencies that make up a sound wave over time. It typically display the frequency spectrum of a sound signal as a two-dimensional image, with time on the x-axis, frequency on the y-axis, and the intensity or power of each frequency component indicated by color or brightness.
